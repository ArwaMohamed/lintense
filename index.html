Together we are changing the world

Welcome to the Lintense, the leading charity offering you a chance to impact lives worldwide. With every charitable project, we prove that even small actions matter.

Learn more

At Lintense, we provide practical, tangible help that makes an immediate difference to people in need worldwide

Lintense Charity steps in where health care ends, providing direct help to people with special needs. For over 30 years, we have ensured that everyone has the support to reach their potential and thrive. Since our foundation, we have distributed more than $30 million in funding to families and organizations in communities.
